How It Works

Three steps from document chaos
to instant, cited answers.

No model training. No cloud lock-in. No months-long implementation. Basin is built for lean teams who need results today.

STEP 01 📂

Drop in your documents

Copy your PDFs — reports, studies, well logs, memos — into a folder. Basin extracts text, chunks it intelligently, and builds a private vector index on your own machine. Your data never leaves your environment.

STEP 02 🔍

Ask in plain English

Type a question the same way you'd ask a colleague. "What's the preferred lateral length in the Wolfcamp?" or "Summarize what we know about fault seal risk on the Eastern Block." No query syntax. No training required.

STEP 03 📎

Get grounded, cited answers

Every answer cites the exact document and page it came from. No hallucinations. No made-up geology. Just your own data, surfaced and synthesized — with a direct trail back to the source so you can verify.
